TOMORROWLAND

The world's biggest electronic music festival

Tomorrowland is an electronic music festival held annually during the penultimate weekend in July in Boom, Belgium.The 180,000 capacity festival features a line up of electronic, EDM and dubstep acts across 15 stages.The ticket price of the festival increases every year with an average price for a weekend ticket of 250 Euros.
