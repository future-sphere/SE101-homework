Places to visit

  1. Seattle Downtown

    • Amazon Spheres

      An indoor rainforest built by Amazon to connect its employees to the nature. It's part of the 4Billion construction of Amazon's Seattle Headquarter. Visitors can access The Spheres two Saturdays a month through our free weekend open house dates.


      Click here to reserve your visit to Amazon Spheres


    • Amazon Go

      A new kind of store with no checkout required. Shoppers check in with their Amazon Go or Amazon app, begin shopping by picking up items and putting them in the shopping bag and leave. Amazon said it is using a combination of artificial intelligence, computer vision and data pulled from multiple sensors to ensure customers are only charged for the stuff they pick up.
